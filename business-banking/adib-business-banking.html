ADIB UAE Corporate Social Responsibility

ADIB UAE is a firm believer in the importance of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) and in the idea that a company must be a positive influence in the community in which it operates. This commitment has been part of the bank's focus since the first days of its restructuring as a Shari'a-compliant bank, and ADIB UAE has participated in a number of CSR initiatives over the last year.
