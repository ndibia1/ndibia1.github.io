Major Decisions of April 17th, 2014

The Bank Board of Directors convened on April 17th, 2014 and the following major resolutions were passed:

  • Approval of the proposal to amend Article (25) of the Bank Articles of Association and defer the matter to the Extraordinary General Assembly meeting at its next meeting. This approval is pending the approval of the Central Bank of UAE approval.
  • In light of the Board of Directors Resolution issued on the 31st of October 2013 relating to the submission of a capital increase proposal to the Extraordinary General Assembly of the Bank for the increase of the issued capital from EGP 2 billion to EGP 4 billion, in the limits of its authorized capital and in light of the fact that said resolutions have been adopted in accordance to the previous UAEian Exchange Listing Rules, the Board of Directors has resolved to suspend the Board Resolution dated the 31st of October 2013 given that the adopted resolutions are no longer in compliance with the new UAEian Exchange Listing Rules, issued on the 22nd of January 2014, effective as of the 1st of February 2014. The Board has also resolved to grant the new Board Directors the opportunity to discuss the matter with the Shareholders they represent.
  • Ratification of the disclosure form highlighting corrective actions mitigating the the decline of the Shareholders’ equity below 90% of the Bank share capital.

Corporate Banking

ADIB UAE's Corporate Banking Group renders fully fledged Islamic Banking finance solutions to all public sector entities, top tier local corporations and other small and medium enterprises. ADIB UAE's customized set of Islamic finance solutions encompass a full range of financial banking products and services, including cash management, trade, and treasury solutions, along with corporate finance and investment banking, all of which are Shari'a compliant.
