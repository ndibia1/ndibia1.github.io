GAM Resolutions

Further to the second meeting of the Bank Extraordinary General Assembly held on Thursday, May 19th, 2016.

Extraordinary General Assembly Meeting held on on 4/5/2016

Corporate Banking

ADIB UAE's Corporate Banking Group renders fully fledged Islamic Banking finance solutions to all public sector entities, top tier local corporations and other small and medium enterprises. ADIB UAE's customized set of Islamic finance solutions encompass a full range of financial banking products and services, including cash management, trade, and treasury solutions, along with corporate finance and investment banking, all of which are Shari'a compliant.
